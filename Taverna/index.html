This Is What Greece Tastes Like

Right under the Acropolis, in the heart of Thissio, our family has been grilling lamb chops and local meats the same way for over 40 years — over real charcoal, with care, simplicity, and flavor that speaks for itself

Ask the locals where to eat — theyll probably send you here. Charcoal-grilled meat, homemade starters, and the kind of place that still cooks with care.
